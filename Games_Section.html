Wandering Ninja

Ready for an adventure?

About:

Wandering Ninja is a small independent game made in Godot during the summer of 2022. This game is a short RPG where you must travel to different areas, complete small quests, and fight enemies along the way.

Project Info:

  • Team Size: 1

  • Time Frame: 2 Months

  • Engine/Language: Godot (gdscript)
